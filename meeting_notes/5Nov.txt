Case 2: (4gen_eigenvals _ 0_x _samex)
Changing E in a dm_M vs theta24 plot only seems to change the amplitude of the dmM oscillations. theta24 changes the frequency. Can see this from the form dm_M \sims E^x*sin(theta24).

Case 1: (4gen_eigenvals _ 0_x _0)
Changing E in a dm_M vs theta24 plot does not show the "only amplitude" behaviour. We have shifting and sometimes completely different behaviour (asymmetric peaks). This only seems to be the case for GeV energies. For TeV, everything flattens out after 3 TeV and is only weakly depending on theta24.
